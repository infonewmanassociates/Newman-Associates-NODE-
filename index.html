Introducing NODE

Your community.

NODE connects local consumers with trusted service providers in their community — built for South Africa, starting in the Swartland.

What is NODE?

NODE is a free mobile marketplace app that helps everyday people find trusted local service providers — plumbers, electricians, hair stylists, caterers, gardeners and more — all vetted and registered through Newman & Associates.

Why NODE?
