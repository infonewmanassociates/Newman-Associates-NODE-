NODE connects local consumers with trusted service providers in their community — built for South Africa.

NODE is a free local marketplace app where consumers find verified, trusted service providers nearby — from plumbers to hairstylists — and providers get discovered by real customers in their own community. Service providers grow their client base without expensive advertising, while consumers save time and avoid the guesswork of finding someone reliable. It's local trust, built into an app.
